Linux 🐧

Linux is an open-source, Unix-like operating system. It’s known for being fast, secure, and
customizable. It powers everything from servers to smartphones (Android), and is a favorite among
developers and IT professionals 🖥️.

Key Features:

  • Free and open-source 🌍
  • Highly customizable and flexible 🛠️
  • Excellent for programming, servers, and security 🧑‍💻🔐
  • Large community support and tons of distributions (Ubuntu, Debian, Fedora, etc.) 🌱

Helpful Resources:

  • Linux Foundation
  • Ubuntu Official Website

macOS 🍏

>macOS features, and seamless integration with other Apple devices, macOS is a favorite for creative professionals

Key Features:
