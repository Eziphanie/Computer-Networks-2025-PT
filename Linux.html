What is Linux?

Linuxis a free, open-source operating system used on computers, servers, phones, and more. It’s fast,
secure, and highly customizable — perfect for both beginners and tech pros.

Key Features of Linux

Easy Interface

Click, drag, and use menus with your mouse or touchscreen.

File Management 📁

Create, save, and organize your files and folders with ease.

Multitasking 🔄

Run multiple apps at once and switch between them quickly.

Security 🛡️

Built-in antivirus, firewall, and updates to keep your PC safe.

⚙️ Hardware Support

Connect devices like printers, cameras, and USB drives instantly.

🌐 Internet & Networking

Easily connect to Wi-Fi, share files, and use the internet.

🛠️ Settings & Customization

Change themes, sound, display, and more to suit your style.

🧩 App Support

Works with thousands of apps — from work tools to games.

🧑‍🦽 Accessibility

Includes tools for users with vision, hearing, or mobility needs.
